Kamal Acharya (कमल आचार्य)

Kamal Acharya (कमल आचार्य; born 27 August 1998) is a Nepalese researcher and author in the field of computer science and information technology (CSIT). His research interests include project management, science and technology, computer engineering, research articles, and Google Analytics.

Early life and education

Acharya was born on 27 August 1998 to father Dilli Raj Acharya and mother Sharada Devi Acharya in Kailali district of Nepal.

He passed School Leaving Certificate (SLC) from Janapriya Secondary School (2014), Likma, Kailali. He completed Intermediate of Science (I.Sc) from Radiant Secondary School (2014–2016), Mahendranagar, Kanchanpur.

He completed Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Information Technology (B.Sc CSIT) from Farwestern University (2016–2019) and Master of Science in CSIT (M.Sc CSIT) from Tribhuvan University (2019–2022).

He is also involved in social work and helping people in need.
