Kamal Acharya (कमल आचार्य)

Born 27 August 1998 – Nepalese researcher and author in computer science and information technology (CSIT)

Biography

Kamal Acharya's research interests are project management, science and technology, computer engineering, research articles, and Google Analytics.

Early Life and Education

Acharya was born on 27th August 1998 to Father Dilli Raj Acharya and Mother Sharada Devi Acharya in Kailali district, Nepal. He completed his School Leaving Certificate (S.L.C) from Janapriya Secondary School (2014), and Intermediate of Science (I.Sc) from Radiant Secondary School (2014-2016) in Mahendranagar, Kanchanpur.

He earned a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (B.Sc CSIT) from Farwestern University (2016-2019), and a Master of Science in Information Technology (M.Sc CSIT) from Tribhuvan University (2019-2022). Acharya is passionate about social work and helping those in need.Later he get married to Babita Baduwal who is from Kanchapur, Nepal.Babita Baduwal is an Nepalese producer, internet celebrity and social worker. She was passed intermediate of management from sunrise public school and passed bachelor of management from Tribhuvan University. She was very clever in her childhood and she was good at student life. She spoke English and Nepali language very well.
