Biography

Kamal Acharya (born 27 August 1998) is a Nepalese researcher and author in computer science and IT (CSIT). His research interests include project management, computer engineering, research articles, and Google Analytics.

Early Life and Education

Kamal was born to Dilli Raj Acharya and Sharada Devi Acharya in Kailali, Nepal. He completed S.L.C from Janapriya Secondary School, I.Sc from Radiant Secondary School, B.Sc CSIT from Farwestern University, and M.Sc CSIT from Tribhuvan University.
