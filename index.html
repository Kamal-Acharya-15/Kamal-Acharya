Biography

Kamal Acharya (born 27 August 1998) is a Nepalese researcher and author in the field of computer science and information technology (CSIT). His research interests include project management, computer engineering, research articles, and Google Analytics.

Early Life and Education

Born to Dilli Raj Acharya and Sharada Devi Acharya in Kailali, Nepal, Kamal passed the School Leaving Certificate from Janapriya Secondary School (2014). He completed intermediate of science from Radiant Secondary School (2014-2016), B.Sc CSIT from Farwestern University (2016-2019), and M.Sc CSIT from Tribhuvan University (2019-2022).

He is also active in social work and helping people in need.
