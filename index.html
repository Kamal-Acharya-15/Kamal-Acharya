Biography

Kamal Acharya (कमल आचार्य) is a Nepalese researcher, technologist, and author known for his work in the field of computer science and artificial intelligence. He has made significant contributions to technology and research in Nepal, specializing in machine learning, IoT (Internet of Things), and data analytics. Kamal is passionate about the integration of modern technology with traditional systems, aiming to improve the quality of life in rural areas through tech-based solutions.

Early Life

Acharya was born on 27th August 1998 to Father Dilli Raj Acharya and Mother Sharada Devi Acharya in Kailali district of Nepal. His curiosity led him to explore the world of programming and software development during his school years. Kamal’s early exposure to technology in Nepal shaped his career path, leading him to pursue higher education in Information Technology.

Education

Kamal Acharya attended the prestigious Farwestern University in Mahendranagar, Nepal, where he completed his Bachelor's degree in Information Technology (2016-2019). Afterward, he pursued a Master's degree in Information Technology from Tribhuvan University, Kathmandu (2019-2022). His master's thesis focused on Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML), with a special interest in improving data analytics for businesses in Nepal. Kamal has attended several workshops and conferences on the applications of AI, IoT, and data analytics in solving real-world problems.

Career

After completing his studies, Kamal started his career as a freelance software developer. He worked with various local businesses, developing solutions tailored to meet the specific needs of each client. His expertise in data analysis, machine learning, and automation made him a sought-after consultant in the tech industry. Kamal later joined Tech Solutions Pvt. Ltd., a leading IT firm in Nepal, where he worked on several high-profile projects involving IoT applications, smart systems, and AI-powered solutions. In addition to his technical work, Kamal has contributed to the Nepalese startup ecosystem by mentoring young developers and sharing his knowledge at technology meetups.

Projects

  • Smart Electricity Bill Payment System: An automated system designed to track, calculate, and manage electricity bills with online payment options.
  • IoT Early Flood Detection System: A system using IoT sensors to detect rising water levels and send early alerts, helping communities avoid flood damage.
  • Stress Detection in IT Professionals: A machine learning-based app that analyzes stress levels among IT professionals, helping to improve workplace wellness.
  • Human Age and Gender Prediction System: AI-based system that uses facial recognition to predict human age and gender, useful in security and demographic analysis.

Publications
